    SLO validation, A/B testing and Progressive Delivery

    Iter8 makes it easy to optimize business metrics and validate SLOs when deploying K8s apps/ML models.

    Quick start in 5 mins

    Highlights

    Statistically rigorous SLO validation and A/B testing

    Integrate with Istio, KFServing, Knative, Seldon ... or any K8s stack of your choice

    Builtin load generation and latency/error metrics collection

    Custom metrics from Prometheus, New Relic, Sysdig, Elastic ... or any database of your choice

    AI-driven: Bayesian analysis for assessing versions and multi-armed bandit for shifting traffic

    Progressive traffic shifting towards the winning version

    Traffic engineering: dark launch, mirroring, user segmentation, and session affinity
